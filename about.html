About Me

I hail from the culturally rich city of Kolkata, India, where my passion for learning has always been a driving force.

Currently, I am immersed in the world of academics at Adamas University, where I am diligently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) degree.

My academic journey is complemented by a diverse array of hobbies that keep me engaged and balanced. I find immense joy in playing various musical instruments, which allows me to express my creativity. Trekking offers me a way to connect with nature and challenge myself physically, while gaming provides a fun and competitive outlet. Additionally, my interest in video editing allows me to blend technology with storytelling, creating compelling visual content.

Professionally, I have had the privilege of working in the corporate field for nearly five years, primarily in media productions. I currently serve as the Lead Post-Production Manager at a renowned wedding company, where I oversee the entire post-production process, ensuring high-quality outcomes. My experience also extends to freelancing, where I have honed my skills and delivered projects across various domains.

Building this website is a testament to my continuous pursuit of knowledge and my commitment to staying ahead in the digital age. It marks another milestone in my career, one that I believe will significantly enhance my professional profile and open new avenues for growth.
